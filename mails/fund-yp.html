Dear YP Committee,

I hope this email finds you well. My name is Nadine Jellali, an IEEE Region 8 member, a part of the IEEE Tunisia Section, and the Secretary of the Tunisian Student and Young Professional (SYP) Congress in its 13th edition, scheduled to take place from December 22nd to 24th, 2025, at Diar El Medina, Hammamet, Tunisia.

The Tunisian SYP is one of the most highly anticipated events in the IEEE community in Tunisia section, especially following the great success of TSYP 11 and TSYP 12. These past editions were truly memorable, and we deeply appreciate your collaboration, which has been instrumental in making them successful. We are honored to work with you again this year to elevate TSYP 13 and make it the best edition yet. I have included in CC the TSYP 11 Chair and TSYP 12 Chair, who strongly recommended having IEEE YP join us again this year with even bigger ambitions, as well as Ms. Madiha, the IEEE Tunisia Section Young Professionals Affinity Group Chair, for further discussion and support.

This year, TSYP 13 is organized by IEEE Tunisia Section, IEEE Tunisia Section chapters and groups, in collaboration with the IEEE ENIS Student Branch. Our vision is to create a global hub where experts, thought leaders, and innovators come together to explore cutting-edge advancements in technology and innovation while envisioning a future grounded in ethical values, sustainability, and social responsibility. We anticipate welcoming over 1,250 engineering students and young professionals to engage, learn, and collaborate on emerging trends shaping the future.

To ensure the success of this event, we are seeking funding assistance from the IEEE Young Professionals Affinity Group through the third cycle of the Meet-up funding program. However, we noticed on the website that the deadline for funding applications is December 12th, 2025. Since our event is scheduled for December 22nd, our application would not be eligible under the current guidelines. We would like to inquire if there is a possibility to submit our application despite the event taking place after the specified deadline.

With more than 200 YP members expected to attend, we aim to foster valuable networking opportunities. For your reference, we have attached the TSYP 12 Report. We are excited about the opportunity to collaborate with you once again and are confident that, together, we can make TSYP 13 a remarkable success.

Additionally, we invite you to access the TSYP 13 VTools link and look forward to your response.

Best regards,
